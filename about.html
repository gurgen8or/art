About Me

Hello, my name is Gourgen Sargsyan. This is my art portfolio where I share all of my favorite work. I enjoy sketching in my sketchbook and I like to experiment with Adobe Photoshop. I am a free thinker, an artist, and a computer wiz. I like to go to the beach, explore the outdoors, and hang out with friends. I grew up in Los Angeles, and I love the weather out here. I also like to travel. My favorite foods are In-N-Out, Sushi, Pizza, Lasagna, Zankou Chicken, and Panda Express. I like all types of music, but my most listened to genres are Hip-Hop and Electronic. I am the oldest of three brothers. I am lucky to be a part of a big family.
